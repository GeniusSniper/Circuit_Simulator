How to use

  • Place parts: pick a component in the palette, then click or drag on the canvas. Terminals snap to the grid — parts connect when their endpoints share a grid point.
  • Wire things up with the Wire tool. Every circuit needs a Ground.
  • Select / Move: click to select, drag to move, drag the blue endpoint handles to reshape. Click a switch to toggle it.
  • Edit values in the Inspector (double-click a part to jump there). Engineering notation works: 4.7k, 10µ, 100n.
  • Watch it run: wire color shows node voltage (green +, red −), moving yellow dots show current flow. Select a part to see its live V/I trace on the mini-scope.

Shortcuts

  • R rotate · Del delete · Esc select tool · Space run/pause · Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y undo/redo
